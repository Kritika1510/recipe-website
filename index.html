Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Take the bread and crush it properly so that it is in very small pieces.
  2. Now, add oil into the bread and mix them properly.
  3. Add the suagr into the mixture and mix them again seamlessly.
  4. now, we get a light mixture of all these and now we have to make balls.
  5. fry all the balls in oil.
